Day 25 — AI Shark Tank Simulator (Claude Challenge)
Overview

This project is part of my 60 Days Claude Challenge, where I build AI-powered, production-ready applications using prompt engineering and full-stack design thinking.

On Day 25, I built an AI Shark Tank Simulator — a fully interactive web application where users can pitch startup ideas and receive AI-driven investor feedback, scoring, and funding decisions.

Project Description

The AI Shark Tank Simulator is a single-page, self-contained HTML application that simulates a real-world startup pitch environment.

Users submit their startup idea, and four AI-powered investor personas evaluate the pitch from different perspectives:

Venture Capitalist (Market & Scalability)
Founder (Execution & Feasibility)
Customer (Usefulness & Value)
Angel Investor (Profitability & ROI)

Each judge asks contextual questions, reacts to user responses, and contributes to a final investment decision.
