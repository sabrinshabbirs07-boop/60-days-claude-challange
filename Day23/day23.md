# Day 23/60 – AI Career Intelligence Platform: Customer & MVP Blueprint

## Overview

As part of my **60 Days of Claude Challenge**, Day 23 focused on validating and planning an AI-powered startup idea through a structured product strategy framework.

Using Claude, I developed a comprehensive **Customer & MVP Blueprint** for an **AI Career Intelligence Platform** designed to help students, graduates, and professionals make better career decisions through personalized AI guidance, skill-gap analysis, and structured career roadmaps.

This project demonstrates how AI can be leveraged not only for content generation but also for startup validation, market research, customer discovery, and product planning.

---

## Project Objective

The goal was to create a data-driven blueprint that answers key startup questions:

* Who are the ideal customers?
* What problems are they facing?
* Which features should be built first?
* How large is the market opportunity?
* What pricing strategy should be adopted?
* What are the major risks and validation milestones?

## Outcome

The analysis identified a promising opportunity in the AI-powered career guidance space while highlighting the importance of:

* Customer validation
* Early traction metrics
* Strong onboarding experiences
* Technical leadership
* Clear product differentiation

The resulting blueprint provides a practical roadmap for moving from idea validation to MVP development.
