06
Key Objections & Buying Triggers
WHY CUSTOMERS BUY
  • Job rejection event — immediate pain triggers action
  • Graduation approaching — urgency to figure out career path
  • Promotion stall — desire to accelerate growth trajectory
  • AI disruption anxiety — fear of role being automated
  • Peer success stories — social proof from community
  • Wasted money on courses — seeking better ROI
WHY CUSTOMERS REJECT
  • "Another subscription I won't use" — price skepticism
  • "Can AI really give career advice?" — trust gap
  • Unknown brand — no social proof at launch
  • Feature paralysis — too complex at first impression
  • "One more thing to set up" — time investment fear
  • Free alternatives — YouTube, Reddit, free tools exist

Counter-strategy: Lead with a single high-value free output (AI career roadmap). Show value before asking for payment. Use success stories as primary social proof from Month 1.

11
30-Day MVP Plan

Week 1: Foundation & Validation (Days 1–7)

  • Freeze single core feature: AI Career Roadmap Generator
  • Conduct 20 structured user interviews with ICP (students + early professionals)
  • Validate willingness-to-pay: ask interviewees for ₹299 pilot commitment letter
  • Map all competitor onboarding flows; identify UX gaps to exploit
  • Shortlist 3 potential technical co-founders / AI/ML engineers to approach

Week 2: Build & Ship (Days 8–14)

  • Ship v0.1: user enters background → AI outputs personalized 90-day skill roadmap
  • Set up analytics (Mixpanel / PostHog): activation, session depth, return visits
  • Launch waitlist landing page with clear value prop and social proof
  • Begin publishing 3x/week on LinkedIn (career insights targeting ICP problems)
  • Send MVP access to 50 hand-curated beta users; gather feedback within 48 hrs

Week 3: Learn & Iterate (Days 15–21)

  • Analyse first-week retention; identify and fix top 3 onboarding dropout points
  • Run "Superhero User" session with 5 power users; capture success story quotes
  • Cold email 10 Tier-2 engineering college placement officers with free pilot offer
  • A/B test two onboarding flows: "goal-first" vs "skill-assessment-first"
  • Begin technical co-founder conversations; set hiring timeline

Week 4: Grow & Decide (Days 22–30)

  • Reach 200 beta users; track 7-day and 14-day retention — target 45%+
  • Convert first 20 paid users at ₹299/mo using "Founding Member" limited offer
  • Compile 30-day report: activation rate, retention, NPS, qualitative feedback themes
  • Present findings to 3 India angel investors / EdTech advisors for feedback
  • Make GO / NO-GO decision: commit to seed fundraise or pivot based on data

Final Verdict
🏆 PROMISING BUT PARTIALLY VALIDATED
Verdict: VALIDATE FURTHER — Investment Committee Decision · June 2025

The opportunity is real, sizeable, and early-innings in India. The AI career intelligence category has strong demand signals and no dominant player. However, the startup is not yet investment-ready due to: (1) absence of traction data, (2) no demonstrated willingness-to-pay at scale, (3) missing technical leadership, and (4) undefined AI differentiation beyond incumbents.

Next 90-Day Target: Ship MVP → 500 free users → 50 paid conversions → 40%+ retention → hire technical co-founder. These four milestones transform this from "validate further" to "GO."
