Defend Your Experience

Your resume got you the interview.
Can you defend it?

An AI interviewer that reads your resume and interrogates you on it — turning passive resume review into active interview defense practice for students and early-career engineers.

01 · Problem

The Problem

Resume tools help students write better resumes — none help them defend what's already on it.

  • Students over-embellish projects and metrics they can't justify out loud
  • Real interviewers probe: "How did you measure that 40%?" "What was your contribution?"
  • The first time this gap surfaces is in a real, high-stakes interview
02 · Target Users

Who This Is For

Primary Users

B.Tech students, fresh graduates, and early-career engineers preparing for campus placements and entry-level interviews.

What They Defend

Projects & GitHub work, technical skills, internships, certifications, academic achievements, problem-solving experience.

03 · Solution

The Solution

Paste your resume. An AI extracts your claims and puts you through a realistic, adaptive, chat-style interview — then gives you a full Defense Report on how you performed.

1. Paste resume
2. AI extracts claims
3. Adaptive chat interview
4. Defense Report
04 · Key Features

Key Features

Claim Extraction

AI reads your resume/projects and identifies exactly what's worth challenging.

Adaptive Interview

5–8 question live chat, escalating depth, ~10–15 minutes — like a real panel round.

Background Scoring

Confidence, technical depth, communication, and evidence strength — scored per answer.

Defense Report

Readiness dashboard + claim-by-claim breakdown + AI coaching summary.

Session History

Local, private history of past sessions to track improvement over time.

Secure by Design

AI API keys stay server-side; resume content never leaves your session insecurely.

05 · Technical Approach

Technical Approach

Frontend

Single-page HTML/CSS/JS app — chat interface, claims review, dashboard, and history screens.

Backend

Lightweight Node.js/Express (or Flask) API layer proxying all AI calls securely.

AI Layer

Claude/OpenAI/Gemini API for claim extraction, adaptive questioning, scoring, and report synthesis.

Storage & Hosting

Browser localStorage for session history; deployed on Vercel/Render.

06 · Future Scope

Future Scope

  • User accounts + cloud sync across devices

  • Company-specific interview simulation modes

07 · Vision

Vision

To become the go-to interview-readiness companion for every student stepping into placement season — not a resume editor, not a quiz app, but a realistic AI interview panel that tells you, honestly, whether you're ready to defend your own story.
