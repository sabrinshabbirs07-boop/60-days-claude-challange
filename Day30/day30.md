# Supply Chain Builder

## Build Smarter. Decide Better. Create a Resilient Supply Chain.

Supply Chain Builder is an interactive supply chain strategy simulation game that helps users understand real-world business decisions through an engaging and practical experience.

Players take the role of a business decision-maker and design a complete supply chain strategy by making important choices across suppliers, manufacturing, logistics, warehouses, and inventory management.

Every decision creates real business trade-offs that affect cost, delivery speed, risk, customer satisfaction, and sustainability.

---

## Project Overview

Modern supply chains require companies to balance multiple challenges:

- Reducing operational costs
- Maintaining fast delivery
- Managing supply chain risks
- Improving customer experience
- Building sustainable operations

Supply Chain Builder transforms these complex concepts into a simple interactive simulation where users learn by making decisions and analyzing their impact.

---

## Features

### Interactive Supply Chain Decisions

Users make five major strategic decisions:

**1. Supplier Strategy**
- Single Supplier
- Multiple Suppliers

Understand the balance between lower cost and supply chain resilience.

**2. Factory Location Strategy**
- Domestic Factory
- Nearshore Factory
- Offshore Factory

Analyze the impact of manufacturing location on cost, speed, and risk.

**3. Warehouse Strategy**
- Centralized Warehouse
- Regional Hubs
- Decentralized Hubs

Design an efficient distribution network.

**4. Transportation Strategy**
- Road
- Rail
- Sea
- Air Freight

Choose transportation methods based on speed, cost, and sustainability.

**5. Inventory Strategy**
- Just-In-Time Inventory
- Balanced Inventory
- Just-In-Case Inventory

Manage the balance between efficiency and safety.
