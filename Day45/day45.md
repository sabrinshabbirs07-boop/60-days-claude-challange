# 🧠 AI Decision Strategist

## Think Better. Decide Smarter.

AI Decision Strategist is an interactive decision-making assistant designed to help users think clearly about difficult choices.

Instead of giving instant answers, it guides users through a structured reflection process, challenges assumptions, identifies biases, evaluates options, and generates a personalized **Decision Report** with actionable next steps.

Built as part of the **ABTalks 60-Day Claude AI Challenge**.

## 📌 Problem Statement

People often struggle with important decisions because emotions, uncertainty, fear of failure, and hidden biases affect their thinking.

Most decision tools simply compare pros and cons, but they fail to:

- Understand the user's real goal
- Identify emotional blockers
- Challenge assumptions
- Measure risks and trade-offs
- Provide a clear action plan

---

## 💡 Solution

AI Decision Strategist acts as an impartial thinking partner.

It uses a structured 4-question interview to understand:

1. The decision and available options
2. The user's goal and timeline
3. Their intuition and hesitation
4. Their biggest fear and reversibility of the choice

After collecting responses, it generates an interactive Decision Report.

# ✨ Features

## 🔹 AI Decision Interview

A guided conversation with exactly four powerful questions:

- What decision are you stuck on?
- What is your goal and timeline?
- What does your gut say?
- What are you afraid of getting wrong?

---

## 🔹 Decision Report Dashboard

Generates a complete visual report containing:

### 1. The Real Decision

Identifies:

- Actual choice being made
- Core trade-off
- Emotional difficulty

---

### 2. Case For Each Option

For every option:

- Strongest argument
- Hidden opportunities
- Biggest weakness
- Best fit values

---

### 3. Assumption Buster

Helps users identify:

- Wrong assumptions
- Decision-making biases
- Ignored factors

Includes common biases:

- Sunk Cost Fallacy
- Loss Aversion
- Status Quo Bias
- Confirmation Bias

---

### 4. Decision Matrix

Evaluates choices across seven dimensions:

| Category | Score |
|---|---|
| Life/Career Upside | /10 |
| Financial Safety | /10 |
| Growth & Learning | /10 |
| Stress Level | /10 |
| Reversibility | /10 |
| Long-Term Alignment | /10 |
| Regret Risk | /10 |

Features:

✅ Animated score bars  
✅ Option comparison  
✅ Winning option highlight  

---

### 5. Premortem Analysis

Imagines failure after 12 months.

Provides:

- Possible failure reasons
- Early warning signs
- Prevention strategies

---

### 6. 7-Day Test Plan

Creates a practical validation roadmap:

- Research
- Experiments
- Conversations
- Decision criteria

---

### 7. AI Verdict

Provides:

- Recommended choice
- Reason behind selection
- What could change the decision
- Hard truth insight

---

### 8. Shareable Decision Cards

Creates screenshot-ready cards:

- Matrix summary
- Final verdict
- LinkedIn sharing card
