# 🛡️ Defend Your Experience

> **Create an AI-Powered Adaptive Interview Defense Simulator**

An intelligent interview preparation platform that transforms your resume, LinkedIn profile, portfolio, research paper, or professional experience into a personalized mock interview. Instead of improving your resume, it helps you confidently **defend every claim** you make about yourself.

---

## 🚀 Overview

Interviewers rarely ask generic questions—they challenge **your** experience.

**Defend Your Experience** is an AI-powered adaptive interview simulator that analyzes your uploaded document and converts every meaningful statement into something you must defend.

Whether it's a project, internship, achievement, research paper, startup, or leadership experience, the AI becomes a realistic interviewer that asks increasingly deeper follow-up questions based on your responses.

The goal isn't to rewrite your resume.

The goal is to ensure you can confidently explain and defend everything you've written.

# 🌟 Why This Project?

Most interview preparation platforms teach users **what to say**.

**Defend Your Experience** teaches users **how to defend what they've already said.**

It simulates realistic interviewer skepticism, helping users identify weak claims, improve storytelling, strengthen technical explanations, and build confidence before facing a real interview.
