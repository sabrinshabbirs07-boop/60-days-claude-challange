# 📑 Solved Case Report

## Case

**CoinWise – Retirement Planning App**

### Root Cause

The campaign allocated most of its advertising budget to TikTok and Instagram Reels despite targeting professionals aged **35–55**.

Although these platforms generated high reach, they failed to attract qualified users.

Meanwhile, LinkedIn and podcast advertising produced significantly better conversion efficiency with a much smaller budget.

---

## Supporting Evidence

- Large budget spent on younger-audience platforms
- Customer feedback indicated audience-channel mismatch
- LinkedIn and podcast campaigns delivered stronger sign-up efficiency
- Campaign optimized for impressions rather than qualified conversions

---

## Recommended Improvements

- Shift budget toward LinkedIn and podcasts
- Prioritize audience-channel fit over maximum reach
- Test channels before scaling campaign budgets
- Measure cost per qualified acquisition instead of cost per click

---

# 📚 Key Learnings

- Marketing is about reaching the **right audience**, not the largest audience.
- High impressions do not guarantee meaningful conversions.
- Customer feedback often reveals hidden campaign issues.
- Data-driven decisions outperform assumptions.
- Successful marketers investigate evidence before making recommendations.
