About the Food Database

NutriScope's food database contains 60 common Indian and global foods. Values are expressed per 100g (or 100ml for liquids) and cover 15 nutrients including macronutrients, dietary fiber, and 9 micronutrients. All values are approximate averages from the sources listed above. Actual values may vary by brand, variety, cooking method, and season.
