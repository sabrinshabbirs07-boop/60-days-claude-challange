# Think Like a Marketing Strategist: Grow This Brand

An interactive, beginner-friendly web application that teaches users **how marketers think**, not just how to create marketing content.

Instead of simply generating marketing ideas, the application walks users through real strategic decisions—from understanding a business to creating a growth strategy and responding to unexpected marketing challenges.

---

## Project Overview

Think Like a Marketing Strategist is designed as an educational simulation where users learn marketing strategy through interactive decision-making.

The application supports three different scenarios:

* Use Your Own Business
* Build Your Personal Brand
* A New Client Has Arrived (Random Business Generator)

Every section explains both **What is this?** and **Why does it matter?**, making the experience suitable for beginners while still being engaging for experienced learners.

---

## Features

### Welcome Experience

* Introduction to marketing strategy
* Beginner-friendly explanations
* Progress tracking
* Modern interactive interface

### Business Modes

* Use Your Own Business
* Build Your Personal Brand
* Random Client Generator

### Audience Research

Learn how marketers identify:

* Target audience
* Pain points
* Goals
* Budget
* Industry
* Competitors
* Marketing challenges

### Platform Strategy

Choose the best marketing platforms based on the audience.

Examples include:

* Instagram
* LinkedIn
* YouTube
* TikTok
* Facebook
* X (Twitter)
* Email Newsletters

Each recommendation includes an explanation of why the platform is or isn't a good fit.

### Content Pillars

Generate multiple strategic content themes and choose the best three.

Examples:

* Thought Leadership
* Audience Education
* Personal Stories
* Behind the Scenes
* Product Education
* Case Studies
* Tutorials
* Community Content

### 30-Day Marketing Roadmap

Generate a high-level weekly strategy covering:

* Week 1
* Week 2
* Week 3
* Week 4

The roadmap focuses on strategic growth instead of individual social media posts.

### Marketing Event Simulation

Users respond to randomized scenarios such as:

* Viral post
* Negative feedback
* Competitor campaign
* Podcast invitation
* Copycat content
* Sudden follower growth

Each decision has consequences that demonstrate strategic thinking.

### Growth Report

At the end of the simulation users receive:

* Audience Understanding
* Platform Strategy
* Content Strategy
* Growth Potential
* Best Decision
* Biggest Mistake
* Three Marketing Lessons

### Prompt Engineering

Every major section includes reusable Claude prompts so users also learn effective AI prompting while studying marketing.

## Project Workflow

1. Choose your mode.
2. Understand the business or personal brand.
3. Analyze the audience.
4. Select marketing platforms.
5. Choose three content pillars.
6. Build a 30-day roadmap.
7. Respond to a marketing event.
8. Review the Growth Report.
9. Learn reusable Claude prompts.
10. Replay with a different business or personal brand.
