# 📰 Media Integrity Analyzer

An interactive, single-file web application that teaches **media literacy through guided discovery**. Instead of testing prior knowledge, the app helps users learn how headlines, emotional language, and audience targeting influence the way information is consumed.

Built with **pure HTML, CSS, and JavaScript**, the entire application works offline without any frameworks, APIs, or external assets.

---

## 🌟 Features

### 📰 Headline Detective

Learn how sensational headlines can misrepresent information.

* Generates fictional news headlines and matching articles
* Decide whether you would click the headline
* Identify exaggerated or misleading language
* Reveal:

  * Headline Accuracy Score
  * Highlighted mismatches
  * Explanation of misleading elements
  * Fair rewritten headline
  * Key media literacy takeaway

---

### 🎭 Emotion Detector

Discover how emotional language shapes online behavior.

* Generates fictional social media posts, reel captions, or article excerpts
* Reflect on how the content made you feel
* Identify emotionally charged words or phrases
* Reveal:

  * Intended target audience
  * Emotional manipulation strategy
  * Highlighted emotional phrases
  * Neutral rewrite
  * Key takeaway

---

### 📊 Live Media Integrity Metrics

Track your progress throughout the experience with live indicators for:

* Headline Accuracy
* Source Reliability
* Emotional Manipulation
* Audience Targeting

---

### 📈 Media Integrity Dashboard

Complete the experience with a personalized learning summary.

Includes:

* Overall Media Integrity Score
* What you learned
* Biggest media red flag detected
* Three practical media literacy habits
* Replay with completely new scenarios
