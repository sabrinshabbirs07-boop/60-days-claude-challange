# Fuel Analysis Dashboard – E85 vs Petrol, Diesel, CNG & EV

## Day 17 of 60 Days Claude Challenge

A professional data analytics dashboard built to compare different fuel types and evaluate the economic and environmental impact of E85 (Flex-Fuel) vehicles.

This project presents fuel cost analysis, CO₂ emission comparisons, break-even calculations, vehicle age impact, and overall fuel efficiency insights through an interactive dashboard design.


##  Project Overview

The dashboard analyzes and compares:

* Petrol
* Diesel
* CNG
* E85 (Flex-Fuel)
* Electric Vehicles (EV)

The objective is to help users understand the trade-offs between fuel cost, environmental impact, maintenance expenses, and long-term ownership costs.

##  Insights Generated

### E85 (Flex-Fuel)

✅ Lower pump price

✅ Reduced emissions compared to petrol and diesel

✅ Environmentally friendlier alternative

❌ Higher running cost per kilometer

❌ Longer refueling time

### EV

✅ Lowest running cost

✅ Lowest emissions

❌ High initial investment

❌ Charging infrastructure dependency

### CNG

✅ Cost-effective

✅ Lower emissions

❌ Limited availability in some regions
