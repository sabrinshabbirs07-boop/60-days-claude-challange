# 🧭 Task Compass

**Learn how work flows through real organizations.**

Task Compass is an interactive, single-file HTML management simulation designed to teach workplace collaboration through hands-on decision making. Instead of testing job titles, it helps users understand how tasks move across teams, who owns different responsibilities, and why collaboration is essential in modern organizations.

Built as **Day 37 of the 60 Days Claude AI Challenge**.

---

## 🚀 Features

- 🏢 Choose from multiple workplace environments
- 🎯 Three interactive learning stages
- 🖱️ Drag-and-drop gameplay
- 👥 Learn ownership and delegation
- 🔄 Build realistic task workflows
- 🤝 Solve collaboration challenges
- 📊 Visual understanding dashboard
- 💡 Personalized learning reflection
- 🎉 Smooth animations and completion celebration
- 🌙 Premium dark glassmorphism interface
- 📱 Fully responsive design
- 📦 Works completely offline

---

## 🎮 Gameplay

### Stage 1 – Who Owns This?
Analyze realistic workplace tasks and assign the most appropriate role responsible for solving the issue.

Learn:
- Ownership
- Responsibility
- Supporting roles

---

### Stage 2 – Task Routing

Build the correct workflow by arranging roles in the order that work should flow through the organization.

Learn:
- Delegation
- Process flow
- Team coordination

---

### Stage 3 – Collaboration Challenge

Handle larger organizational problems by assigning multiple departments to work together.

Learn:
- Cross-functional collaboration
- Communication flow
- Organizational thinking

---

## 📊 Learning Outcomes

Instead of traditional scores, Task Compass measures understanding across:

- Ownership
- Delegation
- Collaboration
- Workflow Thinking

At the end of the experience, players receive personalized insights highlighting strengths and opportunities for improvement.
