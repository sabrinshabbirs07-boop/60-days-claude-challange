# AI-Powered Career Intelligence Platform

## Startup Validation and Market Intelligence Report

## Project Overview

As part of the 60 Days Claude Challenge, Day 22 focuses on building an AI-powered startup validation framework that converts a startup idea into a structured, investor-ready analysis report.

This project demonstrates how Artificial Intelligence can support founders, analysts, and entrepreneurs by providing market research, business analysis, competitive intelligence, and strategic insights.

## Project Objective

The objective of this project is to analyze startup opportunities using AI-based research and structured business evaluation frameworks.

The analysis focuses on:

* Market opportunity
* Customer validation
* Competitive landscape
* Business model evaluation
* Growth potential
* Investment readiness

## Project Concept

The AI-Powered Career Intelligence Platform is designed to help students and professionals make better career decisions through:

* AI-powered career recommendations
* Personalized career pathways
* Skill gap identification
* Real-time market insights
* Opportunity matching
