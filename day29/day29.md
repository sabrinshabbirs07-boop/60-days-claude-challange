# Operation Lifeline: Supply Chain Crisis Lab

An interactive supply chain crisis management simulation game built using React, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

This project transforms complex business decisions into a beginner-friendly simulation where players act as executives managing a company's supply chain during a crisis.

The goal is to understand how real-world decisions impact cost, inventory, customer satisfaction, risk, and business resilience.

---

## Project Overview

Operation Lifeline: Supply Chain Crisis Lab places the player inside an executive War Room.

Players analyze a randomly generated company, manage a supply chain crisis, negotiate with suppliers, make leadership decisions, select AI strategies, and review their final business performance.

Every simulation creates a new experience with different companies, crises, decisions, and outcomes.

---

## Features

### Random Company Generator

Generates fictional companies with:

* Different industries
* Revenue scale
* Factories
* Warehouses
* Suppliers
* Inventory levels
* Lead times
* Global locations

---

### Crisis Simulation

Random business disruptions include:

* Factory Fire
* Supplier Bankruptcy
* Port Strike
* Cyberattack
* Flood
* Raw Material Shortage
* Political Conflict
* Shipping Delay

Each crisis includes urgency level, business impact, and supply chain explanation.

---

### War Room Decision System

Players select three response actions to handle the crisis.

The simulation calculates impact on:

* Cost
* Inventory
* Profit
* Delivery Speed
* Customer Satisfaction

Animated performance updates show how each decision affects the business.

---

### Supplier Negotiation Simulation

A four-round negotiation challenge where players manage:

* Supplier Trust
* Price
* Lead Time

The final negotiation score depends on strategic choices.

---

### CEO Boardroom Challenge

Five leadership questions evaluate:

* Strategic thinking
* Crisis management
* Risk handling
* Executive decision making

---

### AI Strategy Planning

Players choose two AI investments:

* Demand Forecasting
* Inventory Optimization
* Supplier Risk Monitoring
* Warehouse Vision
* Procurement Copilot

The simulation displays expected business improvements.

---

### Final Executive Dashboard

Shows:

* Overall Crisis Score
* Leadership Score
* Negotiation Score
* Resilience Score
* Cost Control
* Risk Management
* Customer Satisfaction

Includes:

* Personalized feedback
* Biggest mistake
* Best decision
* Expert recommendation
* Lessons learned

---

## Tech Stack

* React via CDN
* Babel JSX
* HTML5
* CSS3
* JavaScript

No backend, database, APIs, npm installation, or external assets are used.

The application runs completely offline by opening the HTML file in a browser.

---

## How To Run

1. Download the HTML file
2. Open it in any modern browser
3. Click Start Simulation
4. Review the generated company profile
5. Analyze the supply chain crisis
6. Select response actions
7. Complete supplier negotiation rounds
8. Answer CEO leadership questions
9. Choose AI investments
10. Review final business results
11. Replay with a new scenario
