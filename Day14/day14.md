## Day 14 — Job Red Flag Detector

##  Project Overview

Today I built a **Job Red Flag Detector**, an AI-powered tool that analyzes job descriptions and identifies potential hiring risks before candidates apply.

The goal is to help job seekers make smarter career decisions by highlighting hidden warning signs that are often overlooked in job postings.

##  Problem Statement

Many job descriptions contain subtle red flags such as:

* Unrealistic experience requirements
* Hidden relocation clauses
* Excessive technology stack expectations
* Undefined salary ranges
* Toxic workplace language
* Ambiguous role responsibilities

These signals can lead candidates into roles that don't match their expectations.


## Solution

The Job Red Flag Detector evaluates job descriptions across multiple risk categories and generates an easy-to-understand risk assessment report.

### Key Features

* Overall Risk Score

* Top Red Flag Detection

* Hiring Transparency Analysis

* Remote Work Authenticity Check

*Workplace Culture Evaluation

*Company Stability Indicators

*AI-Generated Interview Questions

*Final Application Recommendation


## Sample Risk Categories

| Category            | Evaluates                      |
| ------------------- | ------------------------------ |
| Requirements        | Experience vs Role Level       |
| Culture             | Toxic Language & Expectations  |
| Remote Authenticity | Hidden Relocation Requirements |
| Hiring Transparency | Salary & Scope Clarity         |
| Company Stability   | Business Maturity & Growth     |


##  Example Red Flags Detected

* "Junior" role requiring 5+ years experience
* Remote role with mandatory relocation clause
* Excessive multi-cloud & multi-language requirements
* 24/7 support expectations hidden in responsibilities
* No salary range disclosed
* Responsibilities spanning multiple seniority levels

##  Why This Matters

Candidates often spend hours applying for jobs that may not align with their career goals.

This tool acts as a first layer of due diligence, helping job seekers:

* Save time
* Avoid misleading opportunities
* Ask better interview questions
* Make informed career decisions
