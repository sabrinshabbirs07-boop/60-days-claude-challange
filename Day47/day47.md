# Day 47/60 – Content Intelligence Studio

## Overview

As part of my **60 Days of Claude Challenge**, Day 47 focused on building an AI-powered content analysis platform for LinkedIn creators and professionals.

**Content Intelligence Studio** is a modern web application that reviews LinkedIn articles through multiple AI-powered reviewer personas. Instead of providing generic suggestions, the platform performs a structured evaluation of content quality, authority, readability, engagement potential, and optimization opportunities before generating actionable recommendations.

The project demonstrates how AI can be integrated into a professional SaaS workflow to assist users in creating higher-quality content.

---

## Features

* AI-powered LinkedIn article analysis
* Multi-stage content review pipeline
* Hook and opening evaluation
* Authority and credibility assessment
* Readability and content structure analysis
* LinkedIn engagement optimization
* Analytics screenshot upload and interpretation
* Personalized rewrite suggestions
* Alternative hooks and article titles
* Publishing checklist
* Professional dark-themed user interface
* Responsive single-page application

---

## AI Review Workflow

The application simulates a panel of AI specialists that review content in sequence:

1. Hook & Structure Analyst
2. Authority & Credibility Reviewer
3. LinkedIn Algorithm Strategist
4. Analytics Interpreter
5. Rewrite & Optimization Coach
6. Executive Editor

Each reviewer contributes insights that are combined into a comprehensive final report.
