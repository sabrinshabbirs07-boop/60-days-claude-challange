* Day 5 – Context Engineering
*Objective
The objective of this task was to understand the impact of Context Engineering by comparing AI-generated outputs with and without context.

*What is Context Engineering?
Context Engineering is the practice of providing relevant information, constraints, goals, and background details to an AI model so it can generate more accurate, personalized, and useful outputs.
In simple terms:
Prompt Engineering = How you ask.
Context Engineering = What the AI knows before answering.

*Prompt A (Without Context)
Create a 30-day learning roadmap.
Include:
- Weekly milestones
- Daily tasks
- Resources
- Projects
- Final outcome
Make it practical and beginner-friendly.
Output Summary:
The generated roadmap was generic and lacked personalization because no information was provided about the learner's goals, skills, experience level, or available study time.

*Prompt B (With Context)
Create a 30-day learning roadmap.
Context:
- Current Situation: Student
- Current Skills: Python Basics, Git & GitHub
- Goal: Become an AI Engineer
- Available Time: 2 Hours per Day
- Experience Level: Beginner
- Preferred Learning Style: Projects & Videos
Include:
- Weekly milestones
- Daily tasks
- Resources
- Projects
- Final outcome
Make it practical and beginner-friendly.
Output Summary:
The generated roadmap was highly personalized and aligned with my learning goals. It included relevant resources, practical projects, and a realistic schedule based on my available time and current skill level.
 Screenshot
![Prompt B Output](https://github.com/sabrinshabbirs07-boop/60-days-claude-challange/blob/main/Day5/ss%20of%20roadmap%20B.jpeg)

#Comparison of Prompt A and Prompt B Outputs

1. Which roadmap feels more personalized?
The roadmap generated using **Prompt B** feels much more personalized. It was created using my current situation, skills, learning goals, available time, experience level, and preferred learning style. Because of this, the roadmap was tailored to my needs and learning journey.
In contrast, **Prompt A** produced a generic roadmap because it lacked any background information about the learner.
2. Which roadmap would you actually follow?
I would follow the roadmap generated by **Prompt B**.
The roadmap aligns with my goal of becoming an AI Engineer, matches my beginner-level experience, and considers my available study time of 2 hours per day. It also includes relevant resources and practical projects that fit my learning style.
Prompt A provides a general roadmap, but it does not take my specific goals or circumstances into account.
3. What role did context play in improving the result?
Context played a crucial role in improving the quality of the output.
By providing information such as:
* Current Situation (Student)
* Current Skills (Python Basics, Git & GitHub)
* Goal (Become an AI Engineer)
* Available Time (2 Hours per Day)
* Experience Level (Beginner)
* Preferred Learning Style (Projects & Videos)

the AI was able to generate a roadmap that was more relevant, practical, and actionable.
Without context, the AI made assumptions and produced a generic response. With context, it created a customized learning plan that better matched my needs and objectives.

*Key Learning
This exercise demonstrated that "Context Engineering significantly improves AI outputs by giving the model the information it needs to generate accurate, personalized, and useful responses."
