## Day 13/60 – Building an AI-Powered Job Discovery Dashboard with Claude AI

# Overview

On Day 13 of my #60DaysOfClaudeAI Challenge, I challenged myself to build a concept of an AI-Powered Job Discovery Dashboard designed specifically for freshers and aspiring data professionals.

Instead of manually scrolling through hundreds of job listings, I explored how AI can analyze a candidate's profile, identify skill gaps, and recommend opportunities that align with their career goals.

The result was a futuristic dashboard that acts like an intelligent career co-pilot.

# Problem Statement

Fresh graduates often struggle with:

- Finding jobs relevant to their skills.
- Understanding which skills recruiters actually expect.
- Identifying gaps in their profiles.
- Knowing which opportunities provide the best growth potential.
- Managing opportunities from multiple platforms.

I wanted to explore how AI could simplify this journey.

# What I Built

An AI-powered job recommendation dashboard featuring:

# Smart Job Discovery

- Personalized role recommendations.
- AI-based job matching.
- Search filters based on:
  - Skills
  - Experience
  - Location
  - Target salary.

## Profile Fit Analysis

- Calculates overall profile strength.
- Estimates job compatibility score.
- Highlights areas that require improvement.

# Skill Gap Analysis

Compares existing skills against market demand and identifies missing competencies such as:

- Power BI
- Tableau
- Deep Learning
- NLP
- Advanced Statistics

# Market Insights

The dashboard also provides insights into:

- Current hiring trends.
- Remote opportunities.
- High-demand skills.
- Salary expectations for freshers.
- Industry requirements from leading companies.
