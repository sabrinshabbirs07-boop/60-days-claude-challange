# Day 08 of 60 Days Claude Challenge

## Personal Environmental Health Analyzer

### Challenge Overview

As part of my **#60DaysClaudeChallenge**, I built a fully interactive **Personal Environmental Health Analyzer** using Claude AI.

This project demonstrates how AI can be used to combine environmental research, data analytics, health insights, and modern dashboard design into a single user-friendly application.

The dashboard analyzes air quality and water quality data, generates environmental health scores, identifies pollution trends, and provides personalized recommendations for healthier living.

---

##  Challenge Goal

Build an AI-powered environmental intelligence dashboard that:

* Collects environmental data
* Analyzes AQI and water quality metrics
* Generates environmental health scores
* Identifies cleanest and most polluted cities
* Provides personalized health recommendations
* Visualizes insights through interactive charts
