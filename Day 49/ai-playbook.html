What this tool does

This is your personal library of reusable AI prompt systems — not a chat app. It stores prompt "workflows" you can reuse, and gives you two builders that assemble sharper prompts from small parts instead of you writing them from scratch each time.

  • Dashboard — browse and reuse your saved AI workflows, grouped by what they're for.
  • Prompt Builder — assemble a one-off prompt from building blocks (role, objective, constraints, etc.) with a live preview.
  • Loop Builder — turn a prompt into a self-checking, repeating instruction with clear stop conditions.
  • My Workflows — all your saved prompts in one searchable list, editable and exportable.

What is the AI Playbook?

It's a personal, offline library of AI prompt systems — reusable structures you fill in and reuse, instead of writing a fresh prompt from memory every time. Everything is saved in this browser via local storage; nothing is sent anywhere.

1

Dashboard — your saved workflows, grouped by purpose (research, planning, scheduling, writing, coding). Click "Use" to fill in a workflow's variables and copy the result.

2

Prompt Builder — add building blocks (role, objective, context, constraints, reasoning strategy, output format, tone, examples, quality checks) to assemble a sharper one-off prompt, with a live preview as you go.

3

Loop Builder — turns any prompt into an autonomous, self-checking loop by adding a goal, evaluation criteria, an improvement strategy, stop conditions, and safety rules.

4

My Workflows — every saved workflow (seeded or your own) in one searchable, filterable list you can edit, duplicate, favorite, or export.
