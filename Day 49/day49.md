# Personal AI Playbook

> Transform your AI habits into reusable workflow systems instead of collecting random prompts.

## Overview

Personal AI Playbook is a premium single-page web application that helps users build a personalized library of AI workflow systems.

Instead of storing hundreds of disconnected prompts, the application focuses on creating modular, reusable workflows that can be combined into thousands of prompt variations. It is designed to help users standardize how they work with AI for writing, coding, research, marketing, learning, business, and more.

The application first interviews the user to understand their profession, goals, preferred AI models, repetitive tasks, productivity bottlenecks, and desired outcomes before generating personalized AI workflow systems.
